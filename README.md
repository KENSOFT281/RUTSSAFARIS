## 🌿 About Ruts Safaris

**Ruts Safaris** is a Kenyan-owned and operated tours & travel company dedicated to curating **authentic, immersive, and unforgettable safari experiences** across Kenya and East Africa. Founded by **Karen Jeptoo**, Ruts Safaris blends deep local knowledge with world-class hospitality standards to deliver journeys that go beyond the ordinary.

We operate under full compliance with Kenya's tourism regulatory framework — including the **Tourism Regulatory Authority (TRA)**, **Kenya Association of Tour Operators (KATO)**, **Kenya Wildlife Service (KWS)**, and the **National Environment Management Authority (NEMA)** — ensuring every adventure is safe, ethical, and sustainable.

> _"We don't just show you Africa. We let Africa speak to you."_
> — **Karen Jeptoo**, Founder & Managing Director


## 🧳 Safari Packages

### 1. 🌄 Classic Kenya Safari *(3–5 Days)*
> The perfect introduction to Kenya's wilderness.

- Maasai Mara game drives (dawn & dusk)
- Full-board accommodation in luxury tented camps
- Professional KWS-certified guide
- Airport transfers from JKIA
- **From KSh 45,000 per person**

---

### 2. 🐘 Big Five Expedition *(7 Days)*
> Hunt the iconic Big Five across multiple ecosystems.

- Amboseli → Tsavo East → Maasai Mara circuit
- Mix of lodge and mobile camp stays
- Maasai village cultural experience
- Sundowner bush dinner
- Hot air balloon safari (optional add-on)
- **From KSh 120,000 per person**

---

### 3. 🦩 Great Migration Spectacular *(8–10 Days)*
> Witness one of nature's greatest spectacles — the annual wildebeest migration.

- Timed to peak migration season (July–October)
- Premium riverside camps in the Mara
- River crossing viewpoint access
- Balloon flight over the Mara plains
- Photography briefing sessions
- **From KSh 180,000 per person**

---

### 4. 🏖️ Bush & Beach Combo *(10 Days)*
> The ultimate Kenya escape — savannah to Indian Ocean.

- Maasai Mara + Amboseli game drives
- Diani Beach or Malindi coastal extension
- Dhow sunset cruise
- Snorkelling & water sports
- **From KSh 200,000 per person**

---

### 5. 🎓 Educational & Research Safari *(Flexible Duration)*
> Designed for schools, universities, and conservation researchers.

- Species survey and habitat assessment walks
- Ranger-led conservation talks
- Community engagement activities
- NEMA-compliant low-impact protocols
- Custom-quoted based on group size

---

### 6. 🌙 Luxury Private Safari *(Fully Bespoke)*
> No shared vehicles. No compromises. Pure exclusivity.

- Private vehicle and dedicated guide
- Exclusive-use camps available
- Michelin-level bush dining experiences
- Spa treatments in the wild
- Helicopter transfers available
- **Price on request**

## 🦋 Conservation Commitment

Ruts Safaris operates on a **Give Back to Nature** philosophy:

- **5% of net revenue** contributed to community wildlife conservancies
- Partnerships with **KWS anti-poaching units** and local Maasai rangers
- **Zero single-use plastics** in all camp operations
- Support for **Grevy's Zebra Trust** and **Save the Elephants** initiatives
- Preference for **locally sourced food** and **community-owned lodges**

> We believe that sustainable tourism is not an option — it is an obligation.
